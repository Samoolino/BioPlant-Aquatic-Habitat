# Disclaimer

This repository documents a speculative bioengineering and biomedical concept.

It does **not** describe a clinically validated medical product, approved therapy, approved implant, or approved nanorobotic system. Nothing in this repository should be interpreted as evidence of safety, efficacy, regulatory clearance, or readiness for human use.

## Intended use of this repository
- Concept exploration
- Early-stage research framing
- Systems architecture documentation
- Risk identification
- Preclinical validation planning

## Not intended for
- Human self-experimentation
- Medical treatment decisions
- Device fabrication for implantation
- Claims of therapeutic performance
- Emergency or rescue use

## Validation status
Current status: **Conceptual / speculative / preclinical framing only**

Any future claim of biomedical validation would require, at minimum:
1. literature support,
2. benchtop testing,
3. material biocompatibility testing,
4. animal studies,
5. human clinical studies,
6. regulatory review.

## Safety note
Because the concept involves invasive or implant-adjacent biomedical ideas, all work should be handled within appropriate ethics, biosafety, engineering, and regulatory frameworks.
