# Research Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Literature & Modeling (0–3 months)
- Validate gas transfer assumptions
- Review oxygen carrier research
- Define realistic performance targets

## Phase 1 — Bench Validation (3–9 months)
- Build membrane test rig
- Measure oxygen transfer rates
- Compare against baseline diffusion

## Phase 2 — Material Compatibility (6–12 months)
- Cytotoxicity testing
- Fluid compatibility
- Stability analysis

## Phase 3 — Integrated Prototype (12–24 months)
- Combine membrane + fluid system
- Controlled environment testing

## Phase 4 — Preclinical Direction (24+ months)
- Define animal study design
- Engage regulatory pathways

## Exit Goal
A validated oxygen-transfer subsystem with measurable improvement over baseline.
